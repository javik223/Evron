Rebooting the market experience

Evron Foodstore is an online market for fresh food produce and groceries. We believe that fresh, healthy and quality food leads to nutritious and delicious meals and we work hard to bring the best to our customers — straight to their door.

The Grand Idea

We envisaged an environment with the aura of the traditional market, but clean, hygienic and without the hassle; a market with warmth and care, devoid of the impersonal and different attitude of the giant industrialized departmental stores.

Our mission is to deliver healthy, hygienic food items and groceries conveniently to our customers at affordable and competitive prices.

We work closely with local farmers to ensure that their products meet our nutrition and cultivation standards, free from hormones and other non-organic additives. We ensure our sourced products are natural and our meat and seafood are produced in the most hygienic environment and meets all safety standards.
